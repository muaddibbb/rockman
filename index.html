Project 01

BLACK NORTH

Raw Emotion. Dark Landscapes. Real Stories.

Black North is an original music project exploring emotional storytelling through a dark fusion of genres. The music moves between fragile acoustic moments and massive emotional climaxes — combining raw guitars, deep vocals, orchestral layers, cold ambience, and haunting melodic structures.

Themes of memory, distance, family, scars, survival, aging, isolation, redemption, and emotional endurance. Every release is designed as part of a larger emotional world rather than standalone singles.

Project 02

FAKE BANGERS

Your Favorite Songs. Completely Rewired.

Fake Bangers is a fictional AI-powered "cover band" that takes iconic songs and completely rebuilds them in unexpected musical styles. Both a tribute and a satire of modern music culture — exploring what happens when familiar songs are pushed into entirely different emotional worlds.

The project openly embraces AI as part of the creative process while focusing on arrangement, atmosphere, reinterpretation, and entertainment.

Project 03

ROCKMAN

Training Music Built Like a Weapon

RockMan is a music and training concept built around the connection between movement, discipline, and heavy music. The channel combines original AI-assisted music production with real training logic, creating structured workout experiences instead of random playlists.

Each session is designed with purpose — whether for strength training, running, rowing, HIIT, warm-up, cycling, warrior training, rugby, or mental focus.

About the Creator

YUVAL JOSEFSON

Yuval Josefson is the creative force behind RockMan, Black North, and Fake Bangers — and the founder of Ze Rock Radio, an independent Israeli rock station and music platform focused on rock and metal culture, original content, and alternative music communities. The result is a connected universe where workouts, albums, fictional bands, visuals, and storytelling all exist under one creative identity.
